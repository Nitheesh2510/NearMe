Yelagiri hills

Yelagiri Hills is one of the hill stations of Tamil Nadu. Yelagiri hill top can be reached by a winding ghat road, that has 14 hairpin bends. The seventh hairpin bend is significant, since it offers the view of the slop of the mountain and the green forest covering as a carpet of the hill.
